PFRA Tracker - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview

PFRA Tracker ("the App") is a fitness assessment calculator and tracker for the Air Force Physical Fitness Readiness Assessment. This App is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the United States Air Force or the Department of Defense.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Data Stored on Your Device

All personal data you enter into the App — including your profile information (age group, sex, height) and fitness test results — is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. If you delete the App, this data is permanently removed.

Data Use

The App uses the information you enter only to calculate and save your fitness assessment results on your device.

No Cross-App Tracking

The App does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location
  • Health or fitness data (all test results remain on your device)
  • Account or login credentials (the App does not require an account)

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics services.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
